Our Mission

The Freedom Air Project provides free, high-quality HVAC systems to veterans in need, restoring comfort, safety, and dignity to those who served our country.

What We Do

Free HVAC Systems

We help veterans facing financial hardship receive reliable heating and cooling systems at no cost.

Community Partnerships

We partner with local HVAC contractors, suppliers, and supporters to make every installation possible.

Veteran Support

Our mission is to improve health, safety, and quality of life for veterans in need.
